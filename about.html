Join our members program in 3 easy steps!


Step 1: Download Metamask Wallet


Go to https://metamask.io/ and click on Download. Choose your preferred browser or mobile application and install the MetaMask extension. MetaMask supports iOS, Android native apps along with Chrome, Firefox, Brave and Edge browser extensions.


Click on the MetaMask extension and click on “Get Started”. You can either import an existent wallet using the seed phrase or create a new one. Click on “Create a Wallet” and create a strong password for your wallet.



Securely store the seed phrase for your wallet.


Never share your seed phrase or your private key to anyone or any site, unless you want them to have full control over your funds.





Step 2: Add Binance Smart Chain Testnet to your network

To add the network manually, open MetaMask, navigate to “Settings” and choose “Network” and click the “Add Network” button





Here are the necessary parameters:
Name of the network: Binance Smart Chain Testnet
URL: https://data-seed-prebsc-1-s1.binance.org:8545
Symbol BNB
Chain ID: 97
Block URL: https://testnet.bscscan.com


Or simply click the button below to add the network automatically





Step 3: Get some free tBnB to transact on the network

Go to https://testnet.bnbchain.org/faucet-smart and enter your metamask wallet address. Click the "Give me BNB" button and you will receive the tBnB within a few minutes.





All Done!


Simply connect your wallet to our website using our "Connect" button and head over to our Accounts page to view your rewards
